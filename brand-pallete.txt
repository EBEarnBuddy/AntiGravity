EARNBUDDY BRAND IDENTITY
================================================================================

DESIGN AESTHETIC: NEO-BRUTALISM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Visual Style:  High contrast, hard lines, geometric shapes, raw feeling.
Core Traits:   2px-4px solid borders, hard shadows (no blur), distinct Z-axis layering.
Vibe:          "Anti-LinkedIn", pro-builder, bold, energetic, confident.
Shadows:       `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px 0px #0f172a` (Hard Offset)
Borders:       `border: 2px solid #0f172a` (Standard) / `4px` (Heavy)

TYPOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Headings:      League Spartan (Variable)
               Usage: H1-H6, Buttons, UI Labels, Hero Text.
               Variable: --font-heading

Body:          Inter (Variable)
               Usage: Paragraphs, Inputs, Metadata.
               Variable: --font-inter

CORE PALETTE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Role            Name            Value      Usage
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Brand Dark      Slate 900       #0f172a    Borders, Text, Hard Shadows
Primary         Green 600       #16a34a    Primary Buttons, Active States
Primary Alt     Green 500       #22c55e    Gradients, Highlights
Canvas          White           #ffffff    Backgrounds, Cards
Surface         Slate 100       #f1f5f9    Secondary Backgrounds

FUNCTIONAL COLORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Role            Name            Value      Usage
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Danger          Red 500         #ef4444    Error messages, Destructive actions
Accent          Purple 600      #9333ea    Feature highlights, Special cards
Warning         Orange 600      #ea580c    Alerts, Warnings
Social          Blue 600        #0077b5    LinkedIn Integration

NOTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Shadows must match "Brand Dark" (#0f172a) to maintain the illustrative look.
* Avoid soft gradients or blurred shadows; keep everything crisp.
* Use "Brand Dark" for all structural borders.
